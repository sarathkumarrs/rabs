About Us

-----------

RABS ENGINEERING WORKS was established in 1976 as a proprietorship firm by Mr M.Sivadasan Pillai, who has wide experience in design and manufacturing of stamping tools & plastic injection moulds for more than Four Decades.

The components manufactured by us are used in Reputed Automotive and General Engineering, Electronics and other industries. We also design and manufacture Plastic injection moulds and press tools for various home appliances, electrical and electronic industries.
